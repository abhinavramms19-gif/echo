Echoes of the Ordinary

Observations of stillness in a moving world.

Somewhere above the clouds
Heart of development of Cochin ports
Frame holding the sea
The waves of solitude washing the shore
Hands of the ports, sky, backwaters and me
Leaves bloom better than some flowers
The intersection of history and the road ahead
If this is a machine then the heart is too
Resting giants in the humid air
The sun, unsure whether to set in this world
